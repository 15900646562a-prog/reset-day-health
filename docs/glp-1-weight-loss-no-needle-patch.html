Understanding GLP-1 and Weight Management

GLP-1, or glucagon-like peptide-1, is a hormone that plays a crucial role in appetite regulation and glucose metabolism. Research suggests that GLP-1 medications can help manage weight by reducing hunger and increasing feelings of fullness. These effects can be particularly beneficial for those struggling with cravings and maintaining a healthy weight.
