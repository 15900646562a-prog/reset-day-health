Cravings and energy slumps can often stem from various factors, including hormonal imbalances and metabolic changes. The Role of GLP-1 in Weight Management

GLP-1 (glucagon-like peptide-1) is a hormone that helps regulate appetite and insulin levels. Studies indicate that medications targeting GLP-1 receptors can lead to weight loss by promoting feelings of fullness and reducing hunger.
