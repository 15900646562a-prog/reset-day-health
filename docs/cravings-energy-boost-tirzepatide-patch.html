Understanding Cravings and Energy Levels

Cravings often arise from hormonal imbalances, stress, or even nutrient deficiencies. Research suggests that fluctuations in insulin and other hormones can trigger hunger signals, leading to unwanted late-night snacking. Additionally, energy crashes in the afternoon can be linked to blood sugar spikes and drops, which are influenced by what you eat during the day.

The Role of GLP-1 in Appetite Regulation

GLP-1 (glucagon-like peptide-1) is a hormone that plays a critical role in appetite regulation and glucose metabolism. Studies indicate that GLP-1 receptor agonists can help reduce cravings and enhance feelings of fullness. T-Patch delivers tirzepatide, a GLP-1 + GIP receptor agonist, through the skin, potentially providing a convenient way to support your weight management goals.
