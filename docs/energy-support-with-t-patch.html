Understanding Energy Crashes

Energy crashes often occur due to fluctuations in blood sugar levels, which can be exacerbated by stress, inadequate sleep, and poor nutrition. When your body experiences these dips, cravings for sugary or high-calorie foods can lead to overeating, especially in the evenings.

Studies indicate that managing these energy dips can help reduce cravings and support weight management. Staying active and maintaining balanced energy levels throughout the day is key.

The Role of GLP-1 in Appetite Regulation

GLP-1 (Glucagon-like peptide-1) is a hormone that plays a crucial role in appetite regulation and energy balance. Medications like Mounjaro and Ozempic work by mimicking GLP-1, helping to reduce hunger and promote a feeling of fullness.
