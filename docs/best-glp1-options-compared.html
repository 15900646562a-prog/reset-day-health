Medvi

Medvi offers a combination of injections and oral tablets featuring compounded semaglutide and tirzepatide. While this option may suit those who prefer a mix of delivery methods, it requires regular injections and an online consultation, which may not be as convenient for everyone.

Hims

Hims provides compounded injections of semaglutide and tirzepatide. Similar to Medvi, this option involves needles and requires a subscription service. While it is a popular choice for many, the necessity of injections can be a dealbreaker for those averse to needles.

Both Henry Meds and Ro primarily offer injections of semaglutide.
