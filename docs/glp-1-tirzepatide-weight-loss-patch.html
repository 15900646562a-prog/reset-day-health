Understanding GLP-1 and Its Role in Weight Management

GLP-1 (glucagon-like peptide-1) is a hormone that plays a significant role in regulating appetite and insulin secretion. Research suggests that activating GLP-1 receptors can help reduce hunger and enhance feelings of fullness, making it easier to manage food intake. By supporting your body’s natural metabolism, GLP-1 medications have become popular tools in weight management.
