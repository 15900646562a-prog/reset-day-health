Understanding GLP-1 and Its Role in Weight Management

GLP-1 (glucagon-like peptide-1) is a hormone that plays a crucial role in regulating appetite and metabolism. Research suggests that GLP-1 receptor agonists, like tirzepatide, can help reduce hunger and improve satiety, making it easier to resist cravings. By mimicking this natural hormone, T-Patch aims to support your body's ability to manage weight effectively.
