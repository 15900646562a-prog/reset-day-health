Understanding GLP-1 and Its Role in Weight Management

GLP-1 (glucagon-like peptide-1) is a hormone that plays a significant role in regulating appetite and glucose metabolism. Research suggests that GLP-1 medications, like Ozempic and Wegovy, can aid in weight loss by enhancing feelings of fullness and reducing cravings. These medications mimic the effects of GLP-1 in the body, helping to manage hunger signals.

However, many people are looking for alternatives to injections, as regular needle use can be inconvenient and uncomfortable.
