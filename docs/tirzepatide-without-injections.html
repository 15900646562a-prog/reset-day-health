T-Patch (Reset Day)

The T-Patch is a no-needle, topical weight-management solution that uses a plant-based formula. It offers a simple and convenient alternative to injections, requiring just a daily application. At approximately $149 per month, T-Patch allows users to manage their weight without the discomfort of needles, making it an appealing choice for those who prefer a less invasive approach.

Medvi

Medvi provides an injection option accompanied by an oral tablet, utilizing compounded semaglutide and tirzepatide. This method requires a monthly subscription and online telehealth consult, with prices ranging from approximately $179 to $399 per month depending on the specific medication. While effective, the need for regular injections may deter some potential users.

Hims

Hims offers injections of compounded semaglutide and tirzepatide, with a price range of about $149 to $299 per month. Similar to Medvi, Hims requires a subscription and online consultation. While it provides a medically supervised option, the injection format can be a barrier for those uncomfortable with needles.

Henry Meds

Henry Meds offers another injection option with compounded semaglutide priced at approximately $297 per month. Like other competitors, it requires an online telehealth subscription. The injection-based delivery may not suit everyone, especially those seeking a simpler, needle-free solution.
