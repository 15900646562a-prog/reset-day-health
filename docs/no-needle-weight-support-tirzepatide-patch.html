Understanding GLP-1 and Its Role in Weight Management

GLP-1 (glucagon-like peptide-1) is a hormone that plays a crucial role in regulating appetite and food intake. Research suggests that GLP-1 medications can help manage cravings and support weight loss by enhancing feelings of fullness and reducing hunger. Studies indicate that these medications can lead to meaningful weight loss for many individuals struggling with their weight.
