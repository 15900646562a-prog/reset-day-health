T-Patch (Reset Day)

The T-Patch offers a convenient, no-needle approach to weight management with its plant-based formula. Users simply apply the patch daily, making it a hassle-free option compared to weekly injections. At approximately $149 per month, it provides a cost-effective solution without the stress of needles.

Medvi

Medvi combines an injection with an oral tablet, utilizing compounded semaglutide and tirzepatide. While it offers a comprehensive approach, the need for injections can be a drawback for those who prefer to avoid needles. Prices range from approximately $179 to $299 per month, depending on the specific treatment.

Hims

Hims provides a similar injection-based treatment with compounded semaglutide and tirzepatide. While it is slightly more affordable, ranging from about $149 to $199 per month, it still requires users to manage weekly injections, which can be inconvenient for some.

Henry Meds

Henry Meds offers an injection option with compounded semaglutide at around $297 per month. Like the other injection options, it may not appeal to those looking to avoid needles entirely, making it less convenient for potential users.

Who Should Pick What
