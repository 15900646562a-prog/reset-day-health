Understanding Cravings and Energy Slumps

Cravings and energy slumps can be linked to various factors, including hormonal imbalances, stress, and diet. Research suggests that fluctuations in hormones, particularly in women, can lead to increased appetite and fatigue. This is especially common for those dealing with conditions like PCOS, where insulin resistance plays a significant role.

By addressing the underlying causes of these issues, you can better manage your cravings and restore your energy levels. Simple lifestyle changes, such as regular exercise and a balanced diet, can make a difference, but sometimes additional support is needed.

The Role of GLP-1 and Metabolism

GLP-1 (glucagon-like peptide-1) is a hormone that plays a crucial role in regulating appetite and glucose metabolism. Medications like Ozempic and Wegovy are designed to mimic GLP-1, helping to reduce cravings and promote weight loss. However, these options often involve injections, which may not be suitable for everyone.
