Understanding PCOS and Insulin Resistance

PCOS affects approximately 1 in 10 women of childbearing age and is characterized by symptoms like irregular periods, weight gain, and difficulty losing weight. Studies indicate that insulin resistance is a key factor in PCOS, making it harder for the body to regulate blood sugar and leading to increased cravings and weight gain. Addressing these underlying issues is crucial for effective weight management.
