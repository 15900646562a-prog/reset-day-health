Understanding GLP-1 and Your Metabolism

GLP-1 (glucagon-like peptide-1) is a hormone that plays a significant role in regulating appetite and glucose metabolism. Research suggests that GLP-1 medications can help individuals manage their weight by promoting feelings of fullness and reducing cravings.
