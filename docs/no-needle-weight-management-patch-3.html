Understanding Cravings and Weight Management

Cravings can be one of the biggest hurdles in maintaining a healthy weight. Research suggests that these urges often stem from hormonal fluctuations, stress, and even sleep deprivation. Addressing these underlying issues is key to achieving lasting weight loss.

For those with conditions like PCOS, insulin resistance can complicate weight management even further. Studies indicate that managing insulin levels is crucial for controlling cravings and supporting metabolic health.

The Role of GLP-1 Medications

GLP-1 medications, such as Mounjaro and Ozempic, have gained popularity for their role in weight management and appetite control. These medications mimic the effects of a naturally occurring hormone that regulates appetite and insulin secretion.
