Understanding Metabolism and Energy Levels

Your metabolism is the process by which your body converts food into energy. Factors such as age, hormonal changes, and lifestyle can influence metabolic rate. Studies indicate that a well-functioning metabolism can help maintain energy levels throughout the day, reducing fatigue and cravings.

The Role of GLP-1 Medications
