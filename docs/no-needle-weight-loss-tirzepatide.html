Understanding Cravings and Metabolism

Cravings can be influenced by various factors, including hormonal fluctuations and emotional triggers. The Role of GLP-1 and Tirzepatide

GLP-1 (glucagon-like peptide-1) is a hormone that plays a crucial role in regulating appetite and glucose metabolism. Tirzepatide, a novel medication that mimics the effects of GLP-1, has been shown to help manage weight by promoting feelings of fullness and reducing cravings.
