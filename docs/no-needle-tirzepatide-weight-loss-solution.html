Understanding GLP-1 and GIP for Weight Management

GLP-1 (glucagon-like peptide-1) and GIP (gastric inhibitory polypeptide) are hormones that play a crucial role in regulating appetite, metabolism, and insulin sensitivity. Research suggests that increasing these hormones can help reduce cravings and promote a feeling of fullness, making it easier to manage your weight.

Tirzepatide, the active ingredient in T-Patch, mimics these hormones to help support your weight loss journey. By using T-Patch, you can experience the benefits of tirzepatide without the discomfort of needles.

How T-Patch Supports Your Metabolism

Metabolism can be influenced by various factors including hormones, age, and lifestyle. Studies indicate that GLP-1 receptor agonists like tirzepatide can enhance metabolic function, leading to better weight management.
