Understanding Metabolism and Energy Levels

As we age, our metabolism naturally slows down, leading to weight gain and decreased energy. Research suggests that hormonal changes, particularly in women, can significantly impact how our bodies process food and manage energy. Conditions like PCOS and insulin resistance can also play a role in these metabolic changes.

Studies indicate that GLP-1 receptor agonists, like tirzepatide, can help regulate appetite, improve insulin sensitivity, and support weight loss. By addressing these factors, individuals may experience a boost in their overall energy levels and mood.
