Medvi offers a mixed delivery method, combining injections with an oral tablet. While this can provide flexibility, the need for injections may deter those looking for a needle-free experience. Hims

Hims also provides an injection-based approach to weight management with compounded semaglutide and tirzepatide. Henry Meds

Henry Meds focuses on compounded semaglutide, available only as an injection. At approximately $297 per month, it is on the higher end of the price spectrum. This option may be suitable for those comfortable with injections but is not ideal for those seeking a no-needle alternative.
