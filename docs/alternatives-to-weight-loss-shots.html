T-Patch (Reset Day)

The T-Patch offers a unique and convenient solution for those who prefer to avoid needles. This topical transdermal patch delivers tirzepatide directly through the skin, eliminating the need for weekly injections. With a straightforward subscription model, it’s easy to manage your weight-loss journey without the discomfort of needles.

Medvi

Medvi provides a combination of injections and oral tablets, offering a flexible approach to weight loss. They use compounded semaglutide and tirzepatide, which can be accessed via telehealth services. While their model is convenient, it still requires weekly injections, which some may find off-putting.

Hims

Hims also offers injections of compounded semaglutide and tirzepatide through a subscription model. They emphasize accessibility via telehealth. However, like Medvi, patients will need to contend with the discomfort of needles each week.

Henry Meds & Ro

Both Henry Meds and Ro provide semaglutide injections through telehealth subscriptions. These options may be appealing to those who prefer a telehealth model, but they too require weekly injections, which can be a barrier for many.
