Medvi

Medvi provides a combination of injection and oral tablet options for semaglutide and tirzepatide. While it offers flexibility, the need for injections may deter some users. Hims offers compounded semaglutide and tirzepatide via injections. Their pricing ranges from approximately $149 to $299 per month, depending on the specific medication. However, like Medvi, the injection format may not appeal to those averse to needles.

Henry Meds provides semaglutide injections at about $297 per month. While it is a straightforward option for those comfortable with needles, it lacks the convenience of a no-needle alternative such as the T-Patch.
