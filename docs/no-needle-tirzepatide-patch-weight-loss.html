Understanding GLP-1 and Its Role in Weight Management

GLP-1 (glucagon-like peptide-1) is a hormone that plays a crucial role in regulating appetite and glucose metabolism. Research suggests that GLP-1 receptor agonists can help reduce cravings and promote weight loss by enhancing feelings of fullness and reducing hunger. This is particularly beneficial for those struggling with weight management or conditions like PCOS, where insulin resistance can complicate efforts to lose weight.
