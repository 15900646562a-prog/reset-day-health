Understanding GLP-1 and Its Role in Weight Management

GLP-1, or glucagon-like peptide-1, is a hormone that plays a crucial role in regulating appetite and glucose metabolism. Research indicates that GLP-1 medications can help people manage their cravings and support weight loss by enhancing feelings of fullness and reducing hunger. Traditionally, these medications have been administered via injections, making them less accessible for some individuals.
