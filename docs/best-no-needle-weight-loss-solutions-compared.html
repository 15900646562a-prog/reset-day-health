T-Patch (Reset Day)

The T-Patch offers a no-needle, once-weekly transdermal delivery of tirzepatide. This innovative patch is designed for those who prefer to avoid injections altogether. With a monthly cost of approximately $149, T-Patch is a convenient and effective option for weight management, delivered through the skin without the hassle of needles.

Medvi

Medvi provides a combination of injections and oral tablets, utilizing compounded semaglutide and tirzepatide. While this option may work for some, it requires regular injections which can be uncomfortable. Prices range from approximately $179 to $399 per month depending on the medication chosen.

Hims

Hims also offers injections of compounded semaglutide and tirzepatide, with costs between $149 and $299 per month. Similar to Medvi, the need for injections can be a deterrent for those who are needle-averse.

Henry Meds

Henry Meds focuses on compounded semaglutide injections, with a monthly price of around $297. While effective, the injection method may not appeal to everyone looking for a more comfortable alternative.
