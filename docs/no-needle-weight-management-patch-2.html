Understanding GLP-1 Medications and Their Role

GLP-1 medications, such as Ozempic, Wegovy, and Mounjaro, have gained attention for their role in weight management. These medications work by mimicking the incretin hormones that your body naturally produces, which help regulate appetite and insulin. Research suggests that GLP-1s can lead to significant weight loss for some individuals. However, weekly injections can be a barrier for many looking for simpler options.

The Challenge of Cravings and Metabolism

Cravings can derail even the most dedicated weight loss efforts. Studies indicate that hormonal fluctuations, particularly in women with conditions like PCOS, can exacerbate these challenges, making it difficult to maintain a healthy weight. Additionally, as we age, our metabolism naturally slows, leading to further complications in weight management.
