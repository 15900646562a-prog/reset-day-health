Understanding GLP-1 and Weight Management

GLP-1 (glucagon-like peptide-1) is a hormone that plays a vital role in regulating appetite and insulin secretion. Research suggests that GLP-1 receptor agonists, such as tirzepatide, can help reduce cravings and promote weight loss by enhancing feelings of fullness and decreasing hunger. This mechanism is particularly beneficial for those struggling with weight management.
