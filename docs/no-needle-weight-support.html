Understanding Tirzepatide and Its Role in Weight Management

Tirzepatide is a dual agonist that targets GLP-1 and GIP receptors, which play crucial roles in appetite regulation and glucose metabolism. Research suggests that these mechanisms can help improve satiety and reduce cravings, making it easier for individuals to manage their weight effectively. By mimicking the body's natural hormones, tirzepatide can support healthier eating habits and enhance metabolic function.
