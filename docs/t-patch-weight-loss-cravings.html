Understanding Cravings and Weight Management

Cravings can often derail your weight-loss efforts. Research suggests that hormonal fluctuations, particularly in women, can influence appetite and cravings. Conditions like PCOS can exacerbate these issues, leading to insulin resistance and increased hunger signals.

The Role of GLP-1 in Weight Loss

GLP-1 (glucagon-like peptide-1) is a hormone that plays a significant role in appetite regulation and glucose metabolism. Studies indicate that GLP-1 receptor agonists like tirzepatide can help reduce cravings and support weight loss by enhancing feelings of fullness and regulating insulin levels.
