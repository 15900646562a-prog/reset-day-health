Quick questions

Is a tirzepatide patch real?

T-Patch is a topical (transdermal) delivery of tirzepatide designed to be applied to the skin once a week instead of injected.

How is it different from the weekly shot?

Same kind of active (tirzepatide, a GLP-1 + GIP agonist), but applied to skin rather than injected — built for people who would rather skip needles.

Do I need a prescription?

Yes. A licensed clinician reviews your profile through a quick online consult and authorises it if it's appropriate for you.

How do I pay?

Cash on delivery (COD) — you pay when your patches arrive.
